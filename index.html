  • About the course

    The Buddhist Correspondence Course is one of the prison programs of the Buddhist Association of the United States (BAUS) and the Phap Ngyuen Congregation that help inmates to learn and apply Buddhist teachings to their lives. Find out more about the Course.

  • About BAUS

    The Buddhist Association of the United States (BAUS) is a non-denominational organization dedicated to promoting the Buddha’s teachings in the US. Read more about its origins, its programs and publications, and its monastic facilities and their services.
